Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, employment details, and any other information you voluntarily provide through our website forms or communication channels.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide recruitment services
  • To match candidates with suitable job opportunities
  • To improve our website and service offerings
  • To communicate important updates and information

3. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your data may be shared only with trusted partners or clients strictly for recruitment and hiring purposes.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. You may disable cookies through your browser settings if preferred.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data by contacting us directly.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
